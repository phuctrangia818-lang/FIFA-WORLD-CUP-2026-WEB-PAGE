📋 About The Project
This is a beginner-friendly HTML/CSS project that showcases key information about the 2026 FIFA World Cup — the largest World Cup in history, featuring 48 national teams.
The page includes:

An overview of the tournament
A schedule table with key dates and locations
National flags of 10 top competing teams
Official logo and ball/trophy imagery
A contact/footer section
Background music (official World Cup 2026 theme)
